I consider myself to be a beginner programmer and always will. I say this because I believe that in the field of programming there is so much to learn and the technology changes so often. I feel that I am always starting from the beginning. I do not think that is a bad thing. I feel that the more I learn about one language or concept it makes it even easier to learn others.
