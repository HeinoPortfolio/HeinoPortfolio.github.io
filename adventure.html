Adventure

Land Activities


We have beautiful rainforests. In the rainforests you can indulge in activities like zip-lining or take in their majestic beauty. Or you can stroll our beautiful beaches where the sand is so white you won't beleive it is real. Another activity that our visitors is hiking our mountain trails.




Ocean Activities


Being located in the Pacific Ocean you can partake in some of the best beaches in the world. We have beaches that are located in and around for individuals that do not like to swim in the waves. If you are daring you can try you hand on the less sheltered beaches. Where you can do things like surf and wake board. If you want to explore the depths of our waters we offer snorkeling around the bay and around the island. From here you can see all the beautiful native aquatic species.


For those who would like to do a little sport fishing. We have a fleet of fishing boats. You can catch large marlin, tuna and other fish species that inhabit our waters. You can book an outting for a half day or the whole.



Sightseeing


Taniti may be a small island but we have a wealth of sighseeing opportunities. In Taniti City you can view our magnificient native architecture. This architecture is very unique to our island and is worth cheking out. The best place to do this is in the area around Yellow Leaf Bay.


Sightseeing can either be done alone or with aid of our bus tours. Our guides will give you an exciting look at the city and the surrounding area. If you are feeling adventurous you can take a ride on one of our helicopter tours. You will be able to see the island from a totally unique perspective.
