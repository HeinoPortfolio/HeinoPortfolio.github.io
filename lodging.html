Lodging

Hotels


We havea four star resort on the isalnd that will cater to your every need. If you stay here you will be pampered like you are the only one in the world. If you would like to stay in more conservative accomadation we have a selection of other hotels that may be of service to you.




Bed and Breakfasts


If you looking for an intimate way to stay. Then our bed and breakfasts may be for you. They offer a very personal touch and you will have the opportunity to interact with your host and learn about the people of Taniti.


For those who would like to do a little sport fishing. We have a fleet of fishing boats. You can catch large marlin, tuna and other fish species that inhabit our waters. You can book an outting for a half day or the whole.



Hostels


If you are looking for a cost-effective alternaltive. We offer the option of staying at a hostel. This money saving option will allow you to spend more money on other activities.
