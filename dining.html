Dining and Other Amenities

Restaurants


The island has 11 restaurants that encompass a wide rangeof cuisine. Although the main fair is based on fish and rice dishes. The restaurants on the island offer other food offerings. We have three restaurants that offer American-style meals. We also have tow tha offer a Pan-Asian cuisine.



Pubs and Clubs


The island also has a dance club that plays all the latest music with large dance floors and a full stocked bar. Along with the night club we have a microbrewery that produces beer that is influenced by the locally available ingredients. We have a few pubs that offer all types of spirits to enjoy.


For those who do not want to attend in our nightlife we have other ammenities. You can watch movies at our local movie theater. The theater plays all the current blockbusters. We offer a bowling alley that is state-of-the-art.



Other Amenities


For your convenience we offer two supermarkets. As wells as two smaller grocery stores that offer more perosnalized service and more island specific items. If you need a quick item you will have access to a 24-hour convenience store where you can stock up on goods.
